Spotify's Year in Music: Drake Most Streamed Artist, Major Lazer & DJ Snake Had Top Song | Billboard

Spotify unveiled its annual Year in Music results on Tuesday, with Drake topping a crowded field to become the world's most streamed artist. The rapper had over 1.8 billion streams and 46 million listeners. The year's most streamed female artist was Rihanna, with over 1 billion streams and 57 million listeners.
